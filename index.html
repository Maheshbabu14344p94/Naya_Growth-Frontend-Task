The Problem

Small poultry farmers lose time, money, and control.

Dependence on external hatcheries

Farmers often depend on outside hatcheries for chicks, which limits independence and planning.

Higher operating cost

Repeated external sourcing can increase expenses and affect margins for smaller farms.

Uncertain production cycles

When control stays outside the farm, timing and batch consistency become harder to manage.

The Solution

A farmer-friendly smart incubator made for local control.

Sere Innovations offers a practical incubation system that helps farmers hatch eggs directly on their own farms. The product combines essential automation with an easy operation model, so users can focus on outcomes instead of technical complexity.

What this changes on the ground

  • Hatching decisions stay with the farmer
  • Less dependency on commercial hatchery schedules
  • Simple repeatable process for each batch
  • More confidence to plan and scale farm activity
